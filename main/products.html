Our Emblem’s Meaning

Our emblem is a direct representation of our work and artistic vision. At first glance, the symbol resembles an animal, paying tribute to the fact that a significant portion of our designs are inspired by wildlife. This unique shape embodies the essence of our brand, making it instantly recognizable.

Symbolism of Beads

Upon closer inspection, the emblem’s ears are composed of bead-like shapes, emphasizing our dedication to intricate beadwork. Every piece we create is carefully woven from beads, making this detail a fitting tribute to our craftsmanship.

Practical Design

The emblem features small attachment loops at the top, resembling those found on keychains. This subtle yet intentional design element highlights the practicality and versatility of our creations.
